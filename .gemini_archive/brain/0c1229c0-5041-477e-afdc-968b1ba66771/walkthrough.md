## What's Left to Download Manually

ExamCooker and CodeChef-VIT are **JavaScript-rendered SPAs** that can't be scraped automatically. These platforms have additional papers (especially from 2023-2026) that must be downloaded manually through a browser:

1. **ExamCooker** (https://examcooker.acmvit.in/) — BMAT202L CAT/FAT papers from 2023-2026
2. **CodeChef-VIT** (https://papers.codechefvit.com/) — Additional BMAT202L papers
3. **Scribd** — Tutorial sheets, quiz papers, additional CATs
4. **VIT Internal Portal** — Campus-only PYQ bank (most comprehensive)

## Key Finding: Saturation Point

After 20+ unique search queries across Google, GitHub, Reddit, Scribd, Studocu, CourseHero, and VIT-specific platforms, the results consistently point back to the **same 4-5 primary sources**. This indicates we've reached the practical limit of what's publicly discoverable online. The remaining papers exist behind:
- ExamCooker/CodeChef-VIT JavaScript interfaces (need browser)
- Scribd paywalls (need subscription for full access)
- VIT campus network (need physical/VPN access)
- Private Telegram/WhatsApp groups (need membership)
