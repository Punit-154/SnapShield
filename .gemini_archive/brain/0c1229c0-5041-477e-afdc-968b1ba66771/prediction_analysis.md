### Question Style Observations
- **Always uses real-world context** (factory workers, students, air coolers, transistors, salesmen, etc.)
- **Data tables are standard** — frequency distributions with 5-8 class intervals
- **Joint distributions** appear in almost every FAT — both discrete (balls/fruits) and continuous (PDF)
- **"Destroyed lab record" regression** is a classic VIT pattern (given regression equations + one variance, find everything else)
- **CO/BL mapping** is standard: CO1 (descriptive), CO2 (probability), CO3 (distributions), CO4 (testing)
