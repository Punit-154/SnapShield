### 1. 📘 Amazon KDP — Coloring Books & Puzzle Books

| Metric | Score | Details |
|--------|-------|---------|
| ⭐ Effort | 4/5 | Low per-book effort once workflow is set up |
| 💰 Income Ceiling | 3/5 | $500–$3,000/mo with 20-50+ quality books |
| ⏱️ Time to First $ | 4/5 | 2-4 weeks after publishing |
| 🖥️ Uses GPU | 5/5 | ComfyUI/Flux generates coloring pages directly |
| 📈 Scalability | 4/5 | Each book is a permanent asset; catalog compounds |

**How Others Do It:**
- Generate 30-50 coloring pages per book using Stable Diffusion/Flux with line-art LoRAs
- Use Book Bolt or Canva for interiors and covers
- Target hyper-specific niches: "Steampunk Animals", "Vintage Botanical Gardens", "Fantasy Mushroom Houses"
- Publish, optimize keywords, move to the next book

**Realistic at 1hr/day:**
- Week 1-2: Research niche + set up ComfyUI workflow (1hr/day)
- Week 3-4: Generate pages + assemble first book (1hr/day)
- **Output: 1-2 coloring books per month** (each earns $20-100+/mo over time)

**Why It's #1:**
- Your GPU does the heavy lifting — generating line-art coloring pages is perfectly suited for 12GB VRAM
- Each book is a permanent income asset on Amazon
- Minimal ongoing maintenance — truly "set and compound"
- Puzzle books (Sudoku, crosswords) can be auto-generated with Book Bolt with almost zero creative effort

> [!TIP]
> **The power move**: Create a *series* of 5-10 related coloring books in the same niche. Amazon cross-promotes books in a series, dramatically increasing visibility.

### 2. 🎨 Etsy Digital Downloads (Wall Art, Printables, Planners)

| Metric | Score | Details |
|--------|-------|---------|
| ⭐ Effort | 4/5 | Batch-create designs, list once, sell forever |
| 💰 Income Ceiling | 4/5 | $1,000–$5,000+/mo with 100+ optimized listings |
| ⏱️ Time to First $ | 3/5 | 2-6 weeks (Etsy SEO takes time to index) |
| 🖥️ Uses GPU | 5/5 | ComfyUI generates all artwork locally |
| 📈 Scalability | 5/5 | No fulfillment costs; each listing is a permanent asset |

**How Others Do It:**
- Generate themed sets of wall art (e.g., "Minimalist Mountain Set of 6", "Vintage Botanical Kitchen Prints")
- Create digital planners, budget trackers, printable calendars
- Bundle into sets for higher perceived value ($4.99-$14.99 per bundle)
- Drive traffic via Pinterest (pin your mockups → link to Etsy)

**Realistic at 1hr/day:**
- Generate 2-3 designs per day using ComfyUI
- Create mockups in Canva (10 min per listing)
- **Output: 10-15 new listings per week**

**Why It's #2:**
- **80-95% profit margins** (no shipping, no inventory)
- Your GPU generates all assets for free
- Pinterest provides free, compounding traffic
- Bundles increase average order value

> [!IMPORTANT]
> **Etsy requires AI disclosure.** Always mention AI assistance in your listing descriptions. Focus on curation and unique creative direction to comply with Etsy's "designed by seller" policy.

## What's Hype vs. Reality

| Claim | Reality |
|-------|---------|
| "Make $10K/month passively with AI books" | Possible but takes 12-24 months with 50+ quality products |
| "Publish a book in 20 minutes" | You can *assemble* one in 20 min, but a *quality sellable* book takes 5-12 hours |
| "Passive income = no work" | It's "front-loaded" work. You build assets that earn passively, but building takes effort |
| "AI does everything for you" | AI handles 70-80% of production, but human curation, niche selection, and quality control are essential |
| "Just upload and money comes" | You MUST do keyword research and niche validation, or you'll publish into the void |
| "GPU rental is free money" | Not with a single mid-range card; electricity costs often exceed revenue |
